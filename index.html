About KisanKart

Connecting farmers directly with consumers for fresh, organic produce

Our Story

KisanKart was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between hardworking farmers and health-conscious consumers. We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, organic vegetables while ensuring farmers receive fair compensation for their dedication.

Founded in 2024, we've grown from a small local initiative to a thriving platform that supports hundreds of farmers and serves thousands of families across the region.

100% Organic

All our vegetables are grown using organic farming methods, free from harmful pesticides and chemicals.

Farm to Table

Direct delivery from farms to your doorstep within 24 hours of harvest, ensuring maximum freshness.

Fair Trade

We ensure farmers receive fair prices for their produce, supporting sustainable agriculture.

Quality Assured

Every product goes through rigorous quality checks before reaching your table.

24/7 Support

Our customer support team is always ready to help you with any queries or concerns.

Easy Ordering

Simple and intuitive platform makes ordering fresh vegetables a breeze.
